
Sizzle Sanctuary Godot 4.3 Project (Milestones 1-3 Ready)
---------------------------------------------------------

1. Download Kenney Roguelike Pack: https://kenney.nl/assets/roguelike-rpg-pack
2. Save chosen tilesheet (16x16, margin 1) as:
   res://art/kenney_roguelike.png
3. Open Game.tscn, select TileMap node, and paint your level.
4. Add collision shapes in TileSet editor to make walls solid.
